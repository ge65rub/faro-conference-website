About the Conference

Sustainability — People, Planet & Progress

The e-studi Komitee Student Conference 2026 brings together students from European universities to tackle one of the defining challenges of our generation: sustainability. Over three days of conference activities in Faro, participants will explore how education, technology, and civic engagement can drive a greener, fairer, and more resilient Europe.

Any student enrolled at a European university is welcome to apply and join. Participation fees are partially covered. Erasmus+ students receive a special discount and 50% accommodation reimbursement.

Public Holiday Planning Note

In Portugal, public holidays are observed on the day they fall and are not moved to Mondays. On Good Friday (3 April) and Easter Sunday (5 April), most shops, offices, and public services will be closed. The conference programme is fully self-contained — meals, transport, and activities are all arranged by the organising team throughout the Easter weekend.
